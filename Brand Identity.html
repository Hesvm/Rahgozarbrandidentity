02 Brand Overview معرفی برند

A quiet companion, not a sales floor.

Rahgozar is a calm, editorial guide for one of the hardest decisions a person makes — leaving home. Every choice in the identity reinforces that the user is in steady hands.

Mission

به مهاجران ایرانی کمک می‌کنیم تا گزینه‌های واقعی خود را با شفافیت و آرامش کشف کنند.

Help Iranian migrants discover their real options — clearly, calmly, and without pressure.

Personality

Steady, literate, attentive. We speak like a thoughtful older sibling — never a sales rep, never a recruiter.

Calm Editorial Precise Warm Honest

Emotional Keywords

Pathways Movement with intention Quiet optimism Emotional clarity Crossing Trust Exploration Safety Compass Horizon

Brand Principles

P · 01

Specifics over claims

Real cost figures, named visa categories, concrete cities. Never "great opportunities await."

P · 02

Calm over excitement

Air-walled hero copy. Short sentences. Editorial rhythm. The brand never raises its voice.

P · 03

One color, lots of stone

Muted oceanic teal as the single accent against warm stone. No secondary brand color, no rainbow status.

P · 04

Persian-first, always

RTL is the default, not a localization. Latin sits underneath Persian as a quiet caption.

P · 05

Drift, never spring

Motion implies floating water. No bounce, no overshoot, no rubber-band physics anywhere.

09 Motion Principles اصول حرکت

Drifting, never springing.

Motion in Rahgozar implies floating water — never rubber. Three primitives cover almost every interaction: drift, fade, and glide.

Drift

±8 px vertical sine loop, 6 seconds. Used on hero marks, illustrations, and the brand boat. Never on functional UI.

cubic-bezier(.22, .61, .36, 1)
رهگذر
Fade

Opacity 0 → 1 with an 8-pixel rise. Default entry for every screen, modal, and section reveal.

240 ms · ease-glide
Glide

Lateral motion for content carousels and chip scrolls. Slow start, slow end — never overshoots.

cubic-bezier(.16, 1, .3, 1)
